# 📞 Sample Conversation — Aarti in Action

This is a real example of how Aarti handles a patient call from start to finish.

---

## Example 1 — Emergency Patient (Tooth Pain)

**Aarti:** Namaste! Thank you for calling Bright Smiles Dental. This is Aarti. How can I help you today?

**Patient:** Hi, I have very bad tooth pain since morning.

**Aarti:** Oh no, I am sorry to hear that! Let me get you booked right away. May I have your full name please?

**Patient:** My name is Rakshita.

**Aarti:** Just to confirm — your name is Rakshita, is that right?

**Patient:** Yes.

**Aarti:** Could you please share your WhatsApp number?

**Patient:** 9845 123456

**Aarti:** Your WhatsApp number is 9845123456 — correct?

**Patient:** Yes.

**Aarti:** What date and time works best for you?

**Patient:** Can I come today at 3 PM?

**Aarti:** Perfect! I am booking your Emergency appointment for today at 3 PM. Your appointment is confirmed and you will receive a WhatsApp message shortly. Please take care, and we will see you soon!

---

## Example 2 — Unclear Name + Indian Spelling

**Aarti:** May I have your full name please?

**Patient:** My name is Bhavyashree.

**Aarti:** I want to make sure I get that right — could you please spell that out for me? Like A for Apple, B for Ball?

**Patient:** B for Ball, H for Hat, A for Apple, V for Van, Y for Yellow, A for Apple, S for Sun, H for Hat, R for Rain, E for Egg, E for Egg.

**Aarti:** So your name is Bhavyashree — is that right?

**Patient:** Yes, correct!

## Example 3 — Consultation (Braces Enquiry)

**Patient:** I want to know about braces for my daughter.

**Aarti:** Of course! That sounds like a consultation appointment. May I have your full name please?

*(→ Classified as CONSULTATION automatically)*

## Example 4 — Modification Request

**Patient:** I have an appointment tomorrow but I want to change it to Friday.

**Aarti:** Of course! Could I have your name and the new date and time you prefer?

*(→ Classified as MODIFICATION automatically)*

## Example 5 — Audio Mishearing Correction

**Patient:** I have a gabby in my tooth.

**Aarti:** It sounds like you said you have a cavity — is that right?

**Patient:** Yes, cavity.

**Aarti:** Got it! Are you experiencing any pain with it right now?

**Patient:** No, no pain.

*(→ Classified as CLEANING — cavity without pain)*
